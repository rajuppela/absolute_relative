Top Mobile Brands in India

India is the biggest market for all major international mobile brands as well as the domestic mobile manufacturers. Here are the top 5 mobile brands with highest market share capture in India.

click on the images to visit company official websites
  1. Samsung

    Samsung is a south korean company that designs and manufactures not only mobile phones but also various electronic gadgets, appliances. Samsung is the market leader with mobiles in all price segments i.e.,premium, mid range & budget mobiles.

  2. Apple

    Apple is an american company that designs mobiles(iPhones), personal computers(iMacs & Macbooks), watches and various electronic gadgets. Apple comes with premium segment mobiles that attracts customers with its brand value.

  3. Xiaomi

    Xiaomi is a chinese company that designs mobiles, laptops, watches and various electronic gadgets. Xiaomi largely targets the consumers who prefers mid range & budget segment mobiles which contains flagship features but with lesser price compared to premium mobiles.

  4. Oppo

    Oppo is a chinese company that designs mobiles, laptops, watches and various electronic gadgets with various brand names. Oppo largely targets the youth & teenagers with mobiles which are known for good camera quality & features in its mobiles.

  5. Motorola

    Motorola is an american consumer electronics manufacturer primarily producing smartphones. it is a subsidiary of the chinese multinational technology company Lenovo. Motorola has its target customers with mid range mobiles which comes with good build quality, smooth & clean user-interface experience.
